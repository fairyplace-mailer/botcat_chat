FairyPlace™ Overview

FairyPlace™ is an international online service specializing in bespoke print and pattern design for fabrics, wallpapers, and leathers.

Our mission is to make custom design accessible, creative, and efficient for clients worldwide.

We operate exclusively online with a distributed team of designers in multiple countries.

Our Values:
- Creativity: Delivering unique and inspiring patterns tailored to each client.
- Accessibility: Offering free design development; clients only pay for production at POD partners.
- Trust: Ensuring privacy, exclusivity, and confidentiality at every stage.

Global Reach: Supporting clients from diverse industries (hospitality, fashion, interior, etc.) worldwide.

Our Process: Order → Sketches → Approval → Private POD link → Production & Logistics by
partner.

FairyPlace™ collaborates with trusted POD platforms including Spoonflower, Contrado, Printful, Society6, and others.

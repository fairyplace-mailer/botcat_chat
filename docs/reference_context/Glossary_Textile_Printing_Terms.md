Glossary of Textile & Printing Terms

Repeat Pattern
A design unit that is duplicated to create a continuous pattern on fabric or wallpaper.

CMYK
Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black — color model used in printing.

RGB
Red, Green, Blue — color model used for digital screens.

Pantone
A standardized color matching system widely used in design and printing.

DPI
Dots Per Inch — measure of print resolution.

Bleed
Extra space around a design to ensure full coverage after cutting.

Warp & WeA
Vertical and horizontal threads in woven fabric.

Selvage
Edge of fabric that prevents unraveling.

Vector Graphics
Graphics based on mathematical paths, scalable without quality loss.

Raster Graphics
Pixel-based images, resolution dependent.

Sublimation
Printing process that uses heat to transfer dye into polyester fabrics.

Screen Printing
Printing technique using stencils to apply ink through mesh screens.

Heat Transfer
Printing method where designs are transferred to fabric using heat and pressure.

Seamless Pattern
A pattern that can repeat infinitely without visible borders.

Swatch
Small fabric sample showing design, color, and quality.

FairyPlace™ POD Partner Policies Summary
Each POD platform has its own policies regarding privacy, visibility, licensing, and order handling.

Spoonflower:
- Offers both public and private listings for designs. - Designers can keep patterns private or publish them for community use. - Clients can order via private links for exclusive use.

Contrado:
- Focus on B2B and premium clients. - Most designs remain private unless the client opts
otherwise.
- Supports NDAs and custom client agreements. Printful: - International POD platform for
a wide range of goods.
- No design marketplace; designs are uploaded per client account.
- Privacy depends entirely on client’s account settings.

Society6:
- Community-driven marketplace.
- Designs are typically public; not optimal for strict exclusivity needs.
- Recommended only for lifestyle/home decor with no exclusivity concerns.

Art Fabrics / Fashion Formula / Woven Monkey:
- Specialized POD services for fabrics and wallpapers. - Support small orders and rapid turnaround.
- Designs typically private, managed directly with client accounts.

In all cases, FairyPlace™ ensures that the safest and most private option is offered to clients when exclusivity is required.
